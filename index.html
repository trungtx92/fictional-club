Overview

We provide engineering and automation solutions for a range of industries, all to support our endeavours towards a more just and equitable society.


Do you have a ‘How do I make it better?’ problem? We can develop a bespoke engineering solution for your use case. After understanding your industry niche, business needs, stakeholders and organisational values, our team of expert engineers shall work with you to provide what you need.

It makes sense to list the stages of delivery so that the client is able to appreciate the flow of our services. The client is not restricted to the whole service set and can opt out at any stage, thus incurring a cost proportional to services provided.

Frequently asked questions

“Can I have a completely mechanical product designed without electronics and computer programming involved?”



Yes. Our team can design a completely mechanical product.



“What does a human rights focused design lab mean?”



SACE design lab has been primarily set up to be a funding source for our not-for-profit venture called Saras.Care. Saras.Care has been set-up to fight the female feticide problem in various parts of the world. Also, we at SACE design labs have a soft corner for designing mechatronic solutions for human rights issues across the world.

“Can I have a product that is not human rights focused designed?”



Yes, you sure can. Our lab is a sister organisation to our human rights initiative called Saras.Care, so we are naturally inclined towards designs that we think support and uphold human rights. However, we are not going to deny you your right of having a beautiful and functional design for your future ambitions (unless the design is intended to cause harm)



“Can I have something like a gun designed? “



No, we’re afraid not. We do not create designs for products intended to cause harm. Before we make a design, we will agree on the use cases that a design is intended for.

INDUSTRIES SERVED

We design and develop solutions to support the Healthcare Industry, one that is heavily reliant on research and development of new products, automation and cost reduction. After assessing a use case, our engineers can conceive and develop an engineering solution. Alternatively, we can work on the client’s concept, to present a visual and animated rendition of the concept, considering real-world applications, materials, mechanical constraints and international standards.

Are you looking for an engineering solution for a human rights problem? Do you need help with designing, developing and testing your idea? If yes, you have come to the right place. We believe that engineering and technology should not be just restricted to certain industries and niches. If you can think of a way to use engineering for solve a genuine Human Rights problem, we can’t wait to hear from you.

As a lab associated with a humanitarian sister organisation Saras.Care, we are naturally inclined to support projects with Human and Social Wellbeing focuses. We are keen to work on concepts around Disability Support, Social Inclusion, Geriatric Wellbeing, Academic Performance and Self-Empowerment. We understand that engineering solutions to social well-being problems need to be tailored to individual requirements, and we are happy to sit with our clients to conceive the best possible engineering solutions, together.

Environmental sustainability is the need of the hour. As an engineering lab of the 21st century, we ourselves are quite environment conscious and follow sustainable practises at our workplace. We welcome projects with the potential to make positive change, with regards to climate change, sustainable oceans, renewable energy and smart energy management.

HOW DOES IT WORk

We accept a project from an organisation/ individual that has an innovative concept or a unique use case for a healthcare application that has an otherwise expensive solution. We believe every human being should be empowered to be healthy, and economic designs facilitate this

The tests of each electronic component that we assemble and programme, will be converted to a readable report, that can be appreciated by the client. Also, the relationship between the mechanical and electronic components will be presented to the component in an easily understandable way.

We run a market study of other Healthcare products for the same or similar use case. Through internal or external consultation, if we are convinced that the client’s concept is practical and economical, or that the use case is genuine, we will move on to look for international and local patents

Following a hybrid agile project management style, each part that we design, analyse and prototype will be tested against the client’s flexible requirements.

Our patent search will be done through large, reputed databases to check if other designs are patent protected. If yes, we will be innovative in our approach and try to deliver smarter designs. Patents will be reviewed by our team of experienced lawyers.

A rendered animated 3d model, with electronics specific movement simulations will be handed over to the client. A detailed report with parts list, program codes, recommended materials to use, cost to produce etc, will also be provided.

We will also explore the likelihood of Healthcare devices of certain kinds being sanctioned in certain countries, so that the client can be more confident about a quicker time to market

Once all the hurdles are out of the way, our team will get on with the design phase
